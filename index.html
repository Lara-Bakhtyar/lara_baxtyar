South Korea

 

 

The Peninsula (including both North and South Koreas) is 1,000 km long and 300 km wide on average, and its total land area is 222,000 km2. The total land area of South Korea (below the Military Demarcation Line) is around 100,364 km2. Around 3/4 of the area is covered with mountains

 

What are 5 facts about South Korea?

  • OFFICIAL NAME: Republic of Korea

  • FORM OF GOVERNMENT: Republic.

  • CAPITAL: Seoul.

  • POPULATION: 51,418,097.

  • OFFICIAL LANGUAGE: Korean.
